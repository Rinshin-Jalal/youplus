# BigBruh Brand Style Guide

## Brand Overview

**Brand Name:** BigBruh
**Tagline:** Your AI Big Brother for The Great Lock-In
**Product:** Daily accountability app with VoIP calling
**Target Audience:** Self-improvement seekers who want aggressive, no-excuses accountability

### Mission
To provide uncompromising, psychological accountability that forces users to confront their lack of progress and eliminate excuses through daily AI-powered reality checks.

### Brand Essence
BigBruh is brutally honest, aggressive, and unforgiving. It's the accountability partner who won't let you off easy, won't accept excuses, and won't let you hide from your commitments. Think of it as tough love meets cutting-edge AI.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors

**Black (#000000)**
- **Usage**: Primary background, text, borders, authority
- **Psychology**: Power, authority, seriousness, no-nonsense attitude
- **Applications**: Headers, main sections, primary text, borders
- **Accessibility**: WCAG AAA compliant with white text

**White (#FFFFFF)**
- **Usage**: Text on dark backgrounds, clean sections, contrast
- **Psychology**: Clarity, truth, revelation, stark contrast
- **Applications**: Text overlays, alternate sections, borders, clean spaces
- **Accessibility**: WCAG AAA compliant with black text

**Red (#EF4444 / red-500)**
- **Usage**: Primary action color, emphasis, confrontation
- **Psychology**: Urgency, danger, intensity, stop-and-think moments
- **Applications**: CTA buttons, highlights, warnings, emphasis text
- **Shades**:
  - Light hover: #EF4444 (red-500)
  - Primary: #EF4444 (red-500)
  - Dark: #DC2626 (red-600)

### Secondary Colors

**Gray Scale**
- Gray-900 (#111827): Near-black for hover states
- Gray-800 (#1F2937): Dark borders
- Gray-700 (#374151): Secondary text
- Gray-600 (#4B5563): Tertiary text, icons
- Gray-400 (#9CA3AF): Disabled states, subtle text
- Gray-300 (#D1D5DB): Light text on dark backgrounds
- Gray-100 (#F3F4F6): Light backgrounds
- Gray-50 (#F9FAFB): Subtle backgrounds

**Accent Colors**

**Green (#4ADE80 / green-400)**
- **Usage**: Success states, social proof, positive metrics
- **Psychology**: Growth, success, achievement
- **Applications**: Success badges, testimonial highlights, stats

**Neon Green (#90FD0E)**
- **Usage**: Special effects, glitch effects, futuristic touches
- **Psychology**: Energy, intensity, digital aesthetic
- **Applications**: Glitch text effects, neon glow effects

**Crimson Red (#FF0033)**
- **Usage**: Special effects, aggressive emphasis, glitch effects
- **Psychology**: Extreme urgency, danger, aggression
- **Applications**: Glitch effects, burn effects, extreme highlights

### Color Usage Guidelines

**Contrast Requirements:**
- Always maintain WCAG AA minimum (4.5:1 for normal text)
- Prefer WCAG AAA (7:1 for normal text) when possible
- Test color combinations for accessibility

**Recommended Pairings:**
- Black background + White text + Red accents
- White background + Black text + Red accents
- Black background + Gray-300 text + Green accents
- Red background + White text + Black borders

**Avoid:**
- Red text on black (low contrast)
- Gray text on gray backgrounds
- Overuse of accent colors (use sparingly for impact)

## Typography

### Font Families

**Impact (Display Font)**
- **Purpose**: Headlines, hero text, emphasis, brand voice
- **Characteristics**: Bold, condensed, commanding, uppercase-friendly
- **Usage**: H1-H6, hero sections, callouts, brand name
- **Fallbacks**: 'Impact', 'Arial Black', sans-serif

**Inter (Body Font)**
- **Purpose**: Body text, UI elements, readability
- **Characteristics**: Clean, modern, highly legible
- **Usage**: Paragraphs, descriptions, UI text, buttons
- **Weights**:
  - Bold (700): Primary body text

- line-height: 1
- letter-spacing: 0.15em

### Typography Guidelines

**Letter Spacing:**
- Impact headings: 0.15em (extra wide tracking)
- Impact callouts: 0.1em (wide tracking)
- Inter uppercase: 0.1em - 0.15em (tracking-wider)
- Inter body: Normal (0)

**Text Transformation:**
- All headings: UPPERCASE
- Buttons: UPPERCASE
- Labels: UPPERCASE
- Body text: Normal case
- Brand name: UPPERCASE

**Font Weight:**
- Primary emphasis: Black (900)
- Secondary emphasis: Bold (700)
- Never use: Regular, medium, or light weights

**Line Height:**
- Headings: 0.85 - 1.2 (tight for impact)
- Body: 1.4 - 1.6 (comfortable reading)
- UI elements: 1.2 - 1.4

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice Characteristics

**1. Brutally Honest**
- No sugar-coating
- Direct confrontation
- Call out failures explicitly
- Use stark language

Examples:
- ✅ "You failed. Again."
- ✅ "That's excuse #6 this month."
- ❌ "Maybe try harder next time?"
- ❌ "You're doing great!"

**2. Aggressive**
- Forceful language
- Imperative commands
- Challenging statements
- No passive voice

Examples:
- ✅ "GET CALLED OUT"
- ✅ "STACK WINS. NOT EXCUSES."
- ❌ "Join our community"
- ❌ "Consider trying our app"

**3. Psychological**
- Reference mental patterns
- Call out self-deception
- Use fear as motivation
- Identity-level language

Examples:
- ✅ "You love recycling trash excuses."
- ✅ "Third dodge this week. You're ghosting accountability."
- ❌ "Please don't forget your goals"
- ❌ "We're here to help you"

**4. Unforgiving**
- Binary language (did/failed, success/failure)
- No middle ground
- Remember everything
- Escalate consequences

Examples:
- ✅ "Did it or Failed. No middle ground."
- ✅ "BigBruh remembers. No forgiveness."
- ❌ "That's okay, try again tomorrow"
- ❌ "We all make mistakes"

### Tone Guidelines by Context

**Hero/Landing**
- Energy: Maximum intensity
- Length: Short, punchy
- Tone: Confrontational challenge
- Example: "BIGBRUH SEES ALL."

**Features**
- Energy: High intensity
- Length: Medium, descriptive
- Tone: Authoritative explanation
- Example: "Every excuse gets stored and thrown back at you."

**CTAs (Call-to-Actions)**
- Energy: Urgent, commanding
- Length: 2-4 words
- Tone: Imperative command
- Example: "GET CALLED OUT"

**Social Proof**
- Energy: Authentic, raw
- Length: Short testimonials
- Tone: Honest admissions
- Example: "Finally stopped lying to myself"

**FAQ/Support**
- Energy: Moderate, still direct
- Length: Clear, concise
- Tone: Matter-of-fact, no apologies
- Example: "No hiding. No quitting."

### Vocabulary

**Power Words (Use These):**
- Confrontation, accountability, reality check
- Aggressive, relentless, unforgiving
- Psychological, fear, identity
- Brutal, raw, stark
- Binary, absolute, no escape
- Called out, exposed, confronted
- Stack wins, eliminate excuses
- Lock-in, commitment, discipline

**Weak Words (Never Use):**
- Gentle, soft, kind, nurturing
- Maybe, perhaps, consider, think about
- Help, assist, support (use "force" instead)
- Community, family, together (individualistic brand)
- Journey, path, exploration
- Try, attempt (demand success)
- Reward, trophy, prize (anti-participation trophy)

## Design Language

### Motion & Effects

**Core Animation Principles:**
- Snappy, hard cuts - no easing, instant state changes
- Aggressive transitions - 200-400ms duration, no bounce
- Glitch effects for phase changes and critical moments

**Special Effects:**

**Glitch Text Effect:**
- Position: relative
- Color: #000
- Animation: glitch-1 0.5s infinite
- Before pseudo: #ff0033 color
- After pseudo: #90fd0e color
- Usage: Hero text, special emphasis (use sparingly)

**Neon Glow:**
- text-shadow: 0 0 5px #90fd0e, 0 0 10px #90fd0e, 0 0 15px #90fd0e
- Usage: Futuristic accents, special callouts

**Burn Effect:**
- background: linear-gradient(45deg, #ff0033, #ff4500)
- -webkit-background-clip: text
- -webkit-text-fill-color: transparent
- Usage: Extreme emphasis, danger zones

### UI Elements

**Rectangular buttons - No rounded corners**
- Border: 2-4px thick (bold borders)
- Solid fills, no gradients except glitch/neon glow
- Minimal iconography - bold/line-based when used
- Single dominant action per screen

## Writing Guidelines

### Content Principles

**1. Be Direct**
- Get to the point immediately
- No warm-up sentences
- Lead with the strongest statement
- Cut unnecessary words

Before: "We think you might find that our app could potentially help you..."
After: "BigBruh forces you to face your failures."

**2. Be Specific**
- Use concrete numbers and examples
- Avoid vague claims
- Reference exact behaviors
- Give tactical details

Before: "We help you stay accountable"
After: "Daily VoIP call at your commitment time. Binary response: Did it or Failed."

**3. Be Confrontational**
- Challenge the reader
- Point out their failures
- Use second person ("you")
- Make it personal

Before: "Many people struggle with accountability"
After: "You're lying to yourself. Again."

**4. Be Binary**
- Success or failure, no middle ground
- Yes or no, no maybe
- On or off, no dimmer switch
- Black or white, no gray

Before: "Track your progress over time"
After: "Did it or Failed. No participation trophies."

### Headline Formulas

**Fear-Based:**
- "YOU'RE [negative action]. HERE'S WHY."
- "THE [number] EXCUSES YOU RECYCLE DAILY"
- "STOP [negative behavior]. START [positive action]."

**Confrontational:**
- "[Action] OR STAY [negative state]"
- "BIGBRUH SEES [your failure]"
- "NO MORE [excuse/behavior]"

**Binary:**
- "[Action] OR [Consequence]"
- "STACK [positive]. NOT [negative]."
- "[Success state] OR FAILURE. NO MIDDLE GROUND."

### Copy Length
- Headlines: 2-8 words, all caps
- Subheadings: 5-15 words
- Descriptions: 15-30 words
- Feature explanations: 20-40 words
- Body paragraphs: 30-60 words max

### Punctuation
- Periods: Absolute statements, facts
- No question marks: We don't ask, we tell
- Minimal commas: Short, punchy sentences
- Colons: Lists, elaboration
- Periods for impact: "Did it. Or failed. No middle ground."

### Capitalization
- Headlines: ALL CAPS
- CTAs: ALL CAPS
- Labels: ALL CAPS
- Body text: Sentence case
- Brand name: BIGBRUH (always caps)

## Brand Do's and Don'ts

### DO
✅ Use all caps for emphasis
✅ Be confrontational and direct
✅ Use red for urgency and action
✅ Maintain high contrast
✅ Keep language binary and absolute
✅ Challenge the user
✅ Use psychological pressure
✅ Reference failures explicitly
✅ Command action (imperative voice)
✅ Keep copy short and punchy

### DON'T
❌ Be gentle or encouraging
❌ Use passive voice
❌ Add unnecessary gradients or effects
❌ Use light font weights
❌ Offer middle-ground options
❌ Use soft language
❌ Add decorative elements
❌ Make excuses for the user
❌ Ask questions (tell, don't ask)
❌ Use participation trophy language
