---

## HOW TO USE THIS TEMPLATE

1. **Complete each section honestly** (self-deception kills startups)
2. **Score each dimension** 0-10 (0 = not started, 10 = exceptional)
3. **Identify gaps** (anything <7 needs immediate attention)
4. **Create action plan** (specific, measurable, time-bound)
5. **Track progress** (compare scores month-over-month)

**Critical Rule:** Don't proceed to amplification (ads, influencers) if foundation scores <7.

### Score Interpretation

**0-4: CRITICAL - Foundation Missing**
- Story unclear or non-existent
- ICP not defined
- Product-first, story-never approach
- **Action:** Stop everything. Define story first.

**5-6: WEAK - Story Exists But Unvalidated**
- Story defined but not tested
- Assumptions, not validated insights
- Segmented narrative (content ≠ design ≠ product)
- **Action:** Validate story before building/launching.

**7-8: GOOD - Story Validated, Ready to Scale**
- Story resonates organically
- Unified narrative across touchpoints
- Conversion proven
- **Action:** Scale with confidence. Amplify validated story.

**9-10: EXCEPTIONAL - Story IS Your Moat**
- Users describe product using YOUR language
- Empathy creates competitive advantage
- Movement, not just product
- **Action:** Maintain consistency as you scale.
