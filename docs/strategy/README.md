## ‚ö†Ô∏è RED FLAGS (STOP IMMEDIATELY IF YOU SEE THESE)

### üö® Red Flag 1: High Views, Low Conversions
**What it means:** Story entertains but doesn't resonate emotionally
**Fix:** Go back to Phase 2 (Story Validation)

### üö® Red Flag 2: Users Don't Use Your Story Language
**What it means:** Story hasn't been internalized
**Fix:** Audit touchpoints for consistency

### üö® Red Flag 3: Spending on Ads Before Organic Works
**What it means:** Amplifying weak foundation
**Fix:** STOP ads. Fix organic story validation first.

### üö® Red Flag 4: Can't Predict ICP Behavior (<70% accuracy)
**What it means:** Don't know them yet
**Fix:** Go back to Phase 1 (Become Your Customer)

### üö® Red Flag 5: Segmented Story Across Touchpoints
**What it means:** Content, design, product tell different stories
**Fix:** Unify narrative. One story, all touchpoints.
